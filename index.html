Who

West Side National Park is managed by the Bahamas National Trust. According to their website, the BNT is a "non-profit, non-government membership organization that relies on donatins to continue conserving and protecting the Bahamian environment." The BNT is managed by a group of 29 members, 14 of whom are elected, and 15 of whom are appointed. The council is home to 6 PhD holders. They are funded by a wide range of both individual and corperate donors such as the Bahamas Telecommunications Company and The Owl Foundation.
